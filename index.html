Our Story

   What is it about Chad that makes him so intelligent? It might have something to do with being from a different planet; believe it or not aliens have a sense of community service similar to humans. Chad’s service is to inform curious people about the United States Naval Academy Computer Science department. This has subsequently made him an expert on the subject of the current classes and professors offered for the Information Technology and Computer Science major. Through the collaborations of a select amount of students and professor advisors. Chad has finally found the platform that he needed to reach our precious underclass. So when you are asking questions please keep that in mind!

How It Works

   Chad has a lot of work to do when it comes to processing all these questions and formulating answers, in a language he is still learning. This is where the work of our capstone team came to the rescue; not only did we create a masterful website that’s easy to navigate and interact with. Chad learned a lot from us using the Word2vec library on python to make matching previously learned questions to the current question asked. Chad uses our database within the Naval Academy Midshipmen server to store all the rules and regular expressions taught to him by the capstone team.
